📋 Project Overview

CodeX is a German beverage company expanding into the Indian market. This project is a comprehensive market analysis of the energy drink segment in India — covering demographics, consumer preferences, competitive landscape, purchase behavior, and marketing effectiveness. The goal is to provide actionable recommendations for CodeX's product positioning and growth strategy.

⚙️ Technologies Used

MySQL Adhoc SQL Analysis ETL Pipeline Excel

👥 Demographic Insights

  • 60% of consumers are male
  • 50% of respondents are between 19–30 years old
  • This increases to 70% when including ages 15–30
  • Online Ads are the most effective channel, reaching 3,373 respondents

☕ Consumer Preferences

  • Caffeine is the top preferred ingredient, followed by vitamins
  • Compact & Portable Cans are in highest demand
  • Innovative bottle designs are the second most preferred packaging
  • Common usage: Sports/exercise, studying, working late nights

🏆 Competition Analysis

  • Cola Coka leads the market, followed by Bepsi
  • Brand reputation is the primary reason for brand preference
  • CodeX brand rating is currently 3.3 out of 5
  • Key focus: cities with high neutral/negative responses

📢 Marketing Channels & Awareness

  • Online Ads are the most effective marketing channel
  • Supermarkets are the preferred purchase location
  • Significant opportunity in cities with neutral/negative brand perception
  • Social media + influencer collaborations are high-ROI channels

🔬 Product Development Areas

📦 Availability

Improve distribution reach across Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities to reduce stockout issues.

🌿 Natural Ingredients

Growing health-consciousness demands a natural ingredients variant with lower sugar content.

🎨 Limited Edition Packaging

Seasonal and limited edition packaging can generate buzz and drive trial purchases.

🗄️ Project Report Gallery

🚀 Recommendations for CodeX

💰 Price Strategy

Price at ₹50–99 in metro cities to match consumer expectations; allow up to ₹150 in premium markets and Tier 1 cities.

📱 Marketing Campaigns

Leverage social media ads, influencer marketing, e-commerce placements, supermarket displays, and event sponsorships.

🎯 Target Audience

Focus all primary campaigns on the 15–30 age group as they represent 70% of the potential consumer base.

🏪 Distribution

Prioritize supermarket shelf presence as the primary touchpoint; expand online availability across major e-commerce platforms.
