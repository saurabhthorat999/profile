📋 Project Overview

The Electric Vehicle (EV) industry has seen rapid transformation over the past few years, driven by technological advancements, government policies, and shifting consumer preferences. This analysis covers the Indian EV market from 2022 to 2024, examining sales trends, manufacturer performance, state-wise penetration, and seasonal patterns to help stakeholders make strategic investment decisions.

💡 Key Insights

🗺️ State Penetration

Top 5 states with highest EV penetration in FY 2024 lead due to strong government policies, infrastructure, and urban adoption rates.

📉 Declining States

Some early-mover states struggled with competition and changing preferences, lacking continuous infrastructure expansion.

📅 Seasonal Trends

Sales peak in Q3 and Q4 (Oct–Mar) due to Diwali and New Year festivals. Q1 & Q2 see slower activity, requiring targeted marketing.

📈 4-Wheeler CAGR

Certain 4-wheeler manufacturers achieved significant growth by focusing on battery technology, affordability, and after-sales services.

🚀 Business Recommendations

  • Strong Growth Potential: Prioritize investments in Goa, Karnataka, and Delhi for expanding EV infrastructure.
  • Seasonal Strategy: Maximize sales efforts in peak months (Oct–Mar); launch festival-season promotions.
  • Competitive Landscape: Ola Electric dominates the 2-wheeler space while Hero Electric struggles — focus on differentiation through battery range and service network.
  • Infrastructure Investment: Key to sustaining growth; expand charging networks in Tier 2 & Tier 3 cities.
  • Battery Innovation: Invest in R&D to reduce costs and improve range per charge to drive mass adoption.
