🏢 Company Background

AtliQ Hardware is a global manufacturer and seller of computer hardware and peripherals including PCs, mice, and printers.

  • Retailer Channels: Brick & Mortar (Croma, Best Buy), E-commerce (Amazon, Flipkart)
  • Direct Channels: AtliQ E-store and AtliQ Exclusive
  • Distributors: Used in restricted trade regions (e.g., Neptune)

🎯 Project Objective

  • Financial Management: Analyzing gross margin and profitability
  • Customer Segmentation: Identifying ideal customers
  • Product Analysis: Evaluating sales performance
  • Inventory Management: Reducing stockouts and overstocking
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Enhancing market strategies

🗄️ Dataset Overview

The dataset comprises 1.8 million records (2018–2022), covering three core areas:

Customer Details

Location, forecast quantity, net sales data across retail and direct channels

Product Details

Categories, manufacturing cost, and revenue metrics per SKU

Market Details

Geographic performance data and operational expenses by region

⚙️ Technologies Used

Microsoft Power BI Desktop Power BI Service DAX MySQL Excel ETL Pipeline Data Modelling

💡 Key Insights

📈 Revenue Growth

Sales grew from $111M to $3.7B between 2019 and 2022 — an extraordinary 33× increase.

🦠 Pandemic Impact

Sales dropped 75% in May 2020 during COVID-19 lockdowns, then rebounded stronger than pre-pandemic levels.

📅 Seasonality

Peak sales occur consistently in November–December each year, driven by holiday purchasing cycles.

🌏 Regional Sales

APAC and North America together contribute 78% of total sales, making them the core revenue markets.

🚀 Recommendations

1
Improve Gross Margin Optimize supply chain forecasting and monitor raw material costs to improve profitability margins.
2
Increase Profitability Focus on high-margin products and improve online retail channel performance.
3
Expand Product Offerings Diversify the product lineup with premium models targeting higher-spending customer segments.
4
Optimize Sales Channels Expand affiliate marketing and influencer partnerships to drive e-commerce growth.
5
Regional Growth Strategies Continue investing in NA & APAC while expanding market presence into LATAM.
